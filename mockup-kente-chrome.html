Kente Crown & Floor

A 3px woven Kente band crowns the top of the window — alternating red, gold, green, black segments replicating an actual Kente cloth edge. Mirrored at the bottom of the status bar at lower opacity. These bookend the entire chrome like the borders of a Kente strip.

Warm Ivory Base

Instead of cold Chrome white (#fff), the light mode uses warm ivory (#fffdf8) with golden-tinted surfaces (#f8f2e5). The header gradient deepens to #faf0d8 — like parchment warmed by Kente gold. High contrast on all text for readability.

Sidebar Thread Edge

The sidebar's right border is a 2px vertical Kente thread — alternating dashes of red, gold, green at 25% opacity. Subtle enough to feel woven, not painted. The sidebar background has faint warm color zones flowing top to bottom.
